Room Baby

Rooms, Broadcasts, File Sharing And More

August 2015 | San Francisco, Ca

Stack: Angular.js, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB

Tools: OpenTok WebRtc API, Grunt, AWS, Underscore, Async

Room Baby enables you to create rooms and broadcasts along with features like file-sharing and hi-def video recording. A room is just like a video chat. You can see and hear them and they can see and hear you. Only these rooms come with a few extra features geared towards live-broadcasting, social-sharing, collaborating and archiving.

As far as Room Baby is concerned, a broadcast is a live audio/video stream of one person. Other people can view the broadcast, but they can't actually join the room as they would in a shared room, sorta like watching tv - only it's a live stream capable of being created by and shared with anyone.
